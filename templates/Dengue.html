DENGUE

Dengue (break-bone fever) is a viral infection that spreads from mosquitoes to people.
It is more common in tropical and subtropical climates. 

Philippines

  • 100,048 cases of Dengue recorded in 2024.
  • From 1 January to 2 December 2023, there have been 657 deaths.
  • DENV 1, DENV 2 and DENV 3 are predominant dengue variants in the Philippines.
