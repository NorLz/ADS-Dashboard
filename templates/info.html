About the Platform

At NOSOS Alert, our mission is to provide real-time tracking of climate-sensitive diseases to help local authorities and the public understand the impact of environmental changes on public health. With the rise in climate-related health risks, such as Dengue, Typhoid Fever, Leptospirosis, and Acute Bloody Diarrhea, it’s crucial to have access to accurate, up-to-date information to act promptly.

Join Us in Making a Difference

At NOSOS Alert, we believe that awareness is the first step toward action. Whether you're a policymaker, healthcare professional, or concerned citizen,
your involvement is vital in addressing the health challenges posed by climate change. Together, we can build healthier, more resilient communities
for generations to come. Our platform not only provides real-time data on climate-sensitive diseases but also equips users with tools to understand trends, predict risks, and make
informed decisions. By bridging the gap between environmental science and public health, we aim to empower communities to take proactive measures against emerging health threats.
